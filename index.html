PeopleForBikes Trade Association

PeopleForBikes is the bike industry’s Trade and Advocacy organization, that regularly demonstrate to members the value of their membership. Representation is the number one membership benefit. As a trade association, PeopleForBikes pushes policies and plans that build a favorable business environment for brand partners and their customers. Membership is essential to fund this work. The Trade Association Homepage links to member-only tools and resources, making it easier than ever to access data, monthly sell-through reports, quarterly risk and opportunity forecasts, national and state-level participations reports, and more.
